    Frequently Asked Questions

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What types of projects does SmartLogicX take on?

    We build CMS platforms, custom business applications, mobile apps, and enterprise systems—including vertical solutions such as e-commerce, hospital, school, hostel, and POS. If you are unsure, send a short brief and we will suggest a sensible first milestone.

    Which technologies do you use most?

    Our teams work with .NET Core, React, React Native, Angular, SQL Server, PostgreSQL, Docker, and cloud services on AWS and Azure—chosen to match performance, team skills, and your hosting preferences.

    How do you ensure quality before release?

    We combine automated and manual testing—functional, regression, API, and performance where needed—alongside code review and staging sign-off so releases are predictable and easier to maintain.

    Can you integrate with our existing systems?

    Yes. Many engagements involve REST APIs, file imports, or database-level integration with legacy software. We document contracts early so both sides know how data flows and how failures are handled.

    Where is SmartLogicX located?

    We are based in Lahore, Pakistan (House #17, Mustafa Town) and work with clients locally and remotely. Use the contact section to reach us by phone or email.
